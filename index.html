About Me

I have a background in economics, where I particularly enjoyed accounting and statistics, but have always been fascinated by the Web as well. That's why in November 2017, I decided to enroll in a year-long in-person bootcamp and have been coding ever since. During the time spent there, I had my first working experience on real projects and it, also, has helped me decide what path should I go in the future career-wise.

Today, I'm part of a couple of communities where I enjoy helping people who are new to coding and online courses that I have already completed as a part of continuing education, which is something I really like about this profession. No one knows everything, and everyone knows something.

As of my design skills, that's something I do from time to time where, in addition to websites related stuff, I usually do logo designs for small businesses in the area I live in.

I'm a communicative and friendly person, living a minimalist lifestyle, but also very analytical when it comes to the things I'm entrusted to do. A perfectionist in some sort of way. I love sports (rather watching it than playing lately, though), good movies and I still listen to the same music I grew up listening to. Besides English, I speak Serbian, which is my native language.
